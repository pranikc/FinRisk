Buyer-dense events for US bank CROs, Treasurers, and Heads of ALM at $10B–$50B institutions:

- **ABA Risk and Compliance Conference** — May 5–7, 2026, Charlotte Convention Center. Booth / sponsor prices in the $20–60K range but attendance-only ~$2K. Primary meeting for community/regional CROs and compliance heads.
- **GARP Financial Risk Symposium** — early March annually in NYC. Next cycle: March 2027. Heavy on quant-risk leaders at larger institutions — good for benchmarking/reputation, weaker for $10B–$50B CRO outreach.
- **ProSight Annual Conference** (the merged RMA + BAI org since 2024) — successor to historic RMA Annual Risk Management Conference and BAI Beacon. Dates vary; check rmahq.org event calendar. Chapter events are cheaper and more local (e.g., RMA Texas Spring Conference Austin, RMA Long Island Credit Roundtable).
- **FDIC Directors' College** — regional, quarterly rotating by FDIC office. Low-cost, high-signal — directors and CROs of community banks attend. Check fdic.gov/regulations/examinations/directorscollege.
- **DCG (Darling Consulting) Balance Sheet Strategy forums** — their own events. Going to a competitor's event is intelligence-gathering, not prospecting.
- **Texas Bankers Association** / state bankers association annual conventions — cheap, packed with in-state CFOs/CROs, especially for Texas/Southeast strategy.

**Why this matters:** With $0 marketing budget and no warm intros, in-person at low-cost regional events converts better than national flagships for a pre-seed.

**How to apply:** Top priority for Pranik's budget — ABA Risk & Compliance 2026 (Charlotte, May 5–7). Second: a state bankers association spring convention in a geography Pranik is targeting. RMA/ProSight national is a 2027 target after whitepaper + first design partner in hand.
